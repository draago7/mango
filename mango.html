About Mango

  • I am mango he king of fruits.
  • Mango is called the "king of fruits" because of its unmatched, rich, sweet-tangy flavor, intense aroma, and superior nutritional value.
  • Mangoes are grown mainly in tropical and subtropical climates,with over 50% of the world's production concentrated in Asia, specifically India, which is the largest producer

About My Varieties

  • Premium Indian Varieties:
  • Alphonso (Hapus): Known for its rich, creamy, non-fibrous, saffron-colored flesh and sweet aroma.
  • Kesar: Famous for its bright yellow pulp and intense sweetness, often used in aamras.
  • Banganapalli (Safeda): Large, yellow, fiberless, and sweet, commonly grown in Andhra Pradesh.
  • Langra: Renowned for its unique lemon-yellow skin, fiberless pulp, and sweet, distinct taste, often from Uttar Pradesh.
  • Chausa: A very sweet and aromatic variety from North India.
  • Totapuri: Tangy, less sweet, and used in salads or pickles.
  • Neelum: High-quality, late-season mango with great keeping quality.
  • Dashehari: A fragrant, sweet variety from Malihabad.
